How I work

My approach

01

Automation-first

If it's done twice, it gets automated. Repeatable infrastructure means fewer surprises and faster delivery.

02

Security by design

Security isn't a phase at the end — it's built into the architecture from the first decision onward.

03

Clear documentation

Every system I touch leaves behind documentation your team can actually use and maintain — written with AI-assisted development in mind, so engineers and AI agents alike have the context to build on it.

04

Best-practice foundations

Proven patterns over clever hacks — solid groundwork that scales with your product instead of fighting it.

05

Less is more

The simpler the better. The best infrastructure is the piece you never had to build — fewer moving parts, fewer surprises, less to maintain.

06

Cost-aware by default

Every architecture decision is made with the bill in mind — right-sized infrastructure that grows with usage, not ahead of it.
